Dignitas Youth is a network of young leaders united for Change.

Youth groups meet at high schools and universities across Canada to raise awareness and funds for our global health programs.

Learn more from Dignitas

These students coordinate advocacy campaigns and events to engage the student community in the global response to HIV and other diseases.
