Who Are We?

Dignitas Youth is a network of young leaders united for change.

Youth groups meet at high schools and universities across Canada to raise awareness and funds for our global health programs. These students coordinate advocacy campaigns and events to engage the student community in the global response to HIV and other diseases.

Our Goals

1. To provide quality of life for the world's most vulnerable populations affected by HIV/AIDS

2. To educate intervention initiatives, current scientific research, socioeconomic issues, and preventative strategies

3. To engage you and the local, national, and international community.

Follow our events

We plan and execute a number of campus events such as seminars, coffeehouses, and fundraisers to promote HIV/AIDS-related issues among the university community.
